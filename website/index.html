About Me

I started my photography career at the age of 12, inspired by the work of Martin Bamford and Tristan Joy. Both are remarkable individuals and photographers. Initially, I used my iPhone XR for photography.

My early published photos were taken at Knowle Park, where I currently volunteer and capture moments. Knowle Park is a 60-acre country park and wildlife reserve, lovingly cared for by a dedicated group of volunteers.
